Welcome to Shutterdome

Shutter what?

Have you ever thought about how cool it would be to have a website where you could submit a photo and have it fight other photos in an online arena where democracy reigns supreme? Then boy howdy did you come to the right place! This is Shutterdome, the rootinest tootinest picture battle website in the entire galaxy!

Why do I care?

If you’ve always wanted to reach superstardom but have a face for radio, then this is the site for you! Post pictures of things that aren’t you, and you could have the chance to achieve virtual fame beyond your wildest binary dreams!

I'm listening...

You the people submit the photos, then they fight other photos by having the world's entire population furiously and ferociously click buttons to vote on them! Every week we declare a winner from each totally sweet category, which change quarterly (that's a fancy way of saying every three months). These gladiators then move on to fight it out battle-royale style at the end of the month against all the weekly winners. From there, each monthly winner goes on to compete for glory in the ultimate Shutterdome battle for all the marbles at the end of the year, where many photos enter, but only one photo leaves!
