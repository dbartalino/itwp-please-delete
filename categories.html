Categories

I'm not sure what to post

Lucky for you, we've got that all figured out! Below are the categories we're currently looking for this quarter to satisfy the god of war...er, the god of picture war, that is.

Video Game Art

Do you like video games? Do you pretend to like art to impress your crush? Then this category is for you! Submit any pictures that are related to video game art. We accept pixel art, character art, level designs, your hand drawn stuff GamePro and Nintendo Power rejected in 1992 and anything else video game related.

Computer Rigs

Sweet rig, bro. Go ahead and take a picture of your computer rigs and submit them for glorious picture battle. We don’t want to see the 'puter your mom bought you for Christmas, we want to see the real deal holyfield that would make Wozniak weak in the knees.

Travel

Been on vacation to somewhere amazing lately, like a cool city or outer space? Annoyed all your Facebook friends by incessantly posting pictures of your excursion? Don’t worry, you can submit travel pictures until your hearts content and we won’t judge you. Until we judge you, and have all the world vote on your pictures!
